Question? We Have Answers!

How to get involve?

You can join our community events, volunteer for projects, or attend barangay meetings to contribute your ideas!

Who leads the barangay?

Our dedicated barangay captain, Harold Beloy, leads our government with a commitment to community service.

What services does the barangay provide?

From waste management to healthcare initiatives, our barangay offers a range of essential services to support our residents.

How do I report concerns or issues?

You can contact our barangay office directly, submit an online form, or attend our regular public feedback sessions.

Can I propose a project for the community?

Absolutely! We welcome creative ideas from residents to improve our barangay and make it a better community!
